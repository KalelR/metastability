Summary / Future

  • Metastability is a crucial dynamical regime for the brain, but a general theoretical framework is still missing.
     
  • Our work proposes such a framework, and thus helps to unify different context-dependent formulations into a general framework to describe and understand metastability by
    • having a general definition that allows for a unifying principle
    • looking deep into dynamical mechanisms
